### Online resources

- [OverSimplified](https://www.youtube.com/@OverSimplified) - Good videos for quick recaps of various historical events, useful for people who are interested in the field and want a starting point.
- [Kings and Generals](https://www.youtube.com/@KingsandGenerals/playlists) - Tons of great documentries on various historical eras and events.
- [Eyes Wide Open](https://www.youtube.com/@eyes_wideopen) - Fantastic documentries on (mostly) US clandestine organizations and operations.
- [UsefulCharts](https://www.youtube.com/@UsefulCharts/playlists) - Fantastic channel if you want visualizations of various historical events, religious sects and more.
- [Whatifalthist](https://www.youtube.com/@WhatifAltHist) - Primarily focused on alt. history (what if xyz happened instead of abc) and geopolitical analysis.
- [Dr. Roy Casagranda](https://www.youtube.com/@DrRoyCasagranda) - Good lectures on modern middle-eastern history.
- [Vinay Lal](https://www.youtube.com/@dillichalo) - Good lectures on ancient and modern-day Indian history.
